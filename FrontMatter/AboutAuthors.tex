\chapter*{About the Authors}

\noindent
\textbf{Samuel Cartwright, Ph.D.} is a Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Natural and Computational Sciences at Fort Valley State University. He holds a Ph.D.\ in Mathematics Education from the University of Georgia and has extensive experience teaching business calculus and mentoring undergraduate students. His work emphasizes student learning, mentorship, and the development of no-cost instructional materials.

\vspace{0.6cm}

\noindent
\textbf{Ishwari J. Kunwar, Ph.D.} is an Associate Professor of Mathematics in the Department of Natural and Computational Sciences at Fort Valley State University. She holds a Ph.D.\ in Mathematics from the Georgia Institute of Technology and conducts research in harmonic analysis, combinatorics, and related fields. His teaching and scholarship emphasize course content development, instructional innovation, and undergraduate research mentoring, with a focus on creating accessible and effective learning experiences for diverse student populations.


\vspace{0.6cm}

\noindent
\textbf{Bhavana Kuntawala Burell} is a Lecturer of Mathematics in the Department of Natural and Computational Sciences at Fort Valley State University. She holds graduate degrees in business, computer science, and education, and her teaching emphasizes foundational mathematics, student support, and the integration of technology in mathematics education.
